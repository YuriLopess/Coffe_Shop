The Reserve Roast was born in Cedar Valley, a serene and picturesque town nestled among lush hills and bathed by the cool ocean breeze. It started as a modest coffee shop founded by Henry, a coffee enthusiast who tirelessly traveled in search of the finest beans around the world.

From the outset, the shop stood out for the exceptional quality of its beans, carefully selected and roasted to offer a unique coffee experience. Henry and his team quickly won over the local community with their unwavering commitment to excellence and passion for the art of coffee. Reserve Roast was not just a store; it was a gathering place for coffee aficionados, where stories were shared and bonds were formed over a cup of the world's most beloved beverage.
